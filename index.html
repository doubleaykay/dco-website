About

The Dartmouth Chamber Orchestra (DCO) is Dartmouth's only student-run orchestra. We choose our own music, conduct, perform, and organize, and we perform anything from classical symphonies to film scores to show tunes. We rehearse every Sunday from 1-3pm in Hartman Rehearsal Hall, and we have a concert at the end of every term!
